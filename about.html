About

Erika Fernando and Damien Wong are two university students at Simon Fraser University (SFU). Both are artists with different styles and have come to work together to make a website to sell their works. Meeting at SFU, the two came together due to a class. As time went on with the project, the two decided to make their works more well known as working alone was doing ok, but not to the degree they wanted. So, they came together and decided to work on art pieces together.
