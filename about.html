Who Are We?

PastelTech is a Canadian company that focuses on making products that have the pastel aesthetic that people have come to enjoy. Our brand focuses on being both stylish and cute. We focus on making quality products that people are able to enjoy wearing and using, enhancing life for people all over the world.

Our Start

This company was founded by Nina Basalt in 2016. She wanted to make clothing that appealed to her sensibilities, as she always found that many clothes, both male and female, were catered towards a certain body type. As such, she wanted to make clothes that people could buy that would match their body type and could even be designed by people for them to try out.
